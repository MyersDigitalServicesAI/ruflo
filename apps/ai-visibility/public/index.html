Transparent methodology

No black box. Every score in your report is computed the same way, and we publish the formula.

We run your buyers' real questions across ChatGPT, Google AI, Perplexity, and Gemini, then score what comes back:

Score = 0.4×(prompt appearance rate) + 0.3×(citation/link rate) + 0.2×(prominence) + 0.1×(sentiment), scaled 0–100

Prominence is the mean of 1/position of your brand's mention across the answers where it appears (first-mentioned = 1.0). Sentiment maps positive = 1, neutral = 0.5, negative = 0. AI answers vary run to run, so scores are directional and we report changes over time rather than false precision.
